## My Account

### How do I create an account?
Click "Register" in the nav bar:
- Sign up with email
- Or use Google/GitHub OAuth
- Verify your email
- Start browsing!

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to Login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check your inbox
5. Click reset link
6. Create new password

### How do I update my profile?
1. Login to your account
2. Go to Profile page
3. Edit name, email, phone, company, bio
4. Click Save
Your changes are saved automatically.

## Travel Bookings

### How do I book a trip?
Book your trip:
1. Go to Travel section
2. Browse destinations
3. Click "Book Now"
4. Select dates & guests
5. Enter details
6. Confirm & pay

### Can I modify my booking?
Modify your booking:
- Contact 48+ hours before check-in
- Change dates or guest count
- Update special requests
- Subject to availability

### What's included in travel packages?
Each package includes:
- Accommodation
- Meals (as specified)
- Airport transfers
- Guided tours
- Activities
Check destination for details.
